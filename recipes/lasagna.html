Lasagna

A traditional and tasty lasagna for anyone to enjoy

This is a recipe that has been passed on from Sicilian immigrants for decades. Whenever you think of Italy, Super Mario and Ferrari, This dish will represent that authentic Italian flavor.

In addition to that original, homecooked flavor, it is rather easy to make!

Ingredients
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
    •
    Steps
    1. Boil tomatoes
    2. Add salt and pepper to ground beef
    3. Boil pasta
