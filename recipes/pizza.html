Pizza

The best pizza ever!

Forget Domino's and Pizza Hut, this is where it's at.

This authentic Margherita pizza is a taste explosion.

Ingredients
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Bread
Steps
  1. Pour a layer of tomato sauce over bread with some oil on it
  2. Take cheese and spread it over
  3. Shove it into the oven
