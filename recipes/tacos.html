Tacos

Ready for some Mexican tacos?

Tacos are everywhere, but not all of them are real tacos...

Let's make some authentic ones! Viva Mexico!

Ingredients
  • Tortilla
  • Steak
  • Vegetables
Steps
  1. Place tortillas on heated pan
  2. Dice meat and cook alongside tortillas
  3. Sprinkle diced vegetables over tacos
